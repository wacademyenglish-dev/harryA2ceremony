The Great Hall

Welcome to the magic school. Look up at the ceiling. There are hundreds of candles floating in the air. The students are nervous. Today is a very important day.

The Hat Wants to Know You

Every new student must wear the magical hat. The hat looks inside your head to see who you really are.

Let's find out your house. Answer 3 questions.

Question 1

Question 2

Question 3

New Word: Brave

"Gryffindor students are very BRAVE."

When you are brave, you do things even if they are difficult or scary.

New Word: Smart

"Ravenclaw students are SMART."

They love to read books, learn new things, and answer difficult questions.

New Word: Bad

"Not every Slytherin is a BAD wizard."

Sometimes, people think they are evil, but they just want to win.

New Word: Scared

"The young boy looked very SCARED."

When you are scared, your heart beats fast and you want to hide.

Check Your Spells

Fill in the blanks. You have 3 tries.

"Harry is not _________. He is very _________."

Grammar: The Future

The Hat looks at the students and makes a prediction about their future.

To make a prediction, we use:
WILL + Verb

Practice 'Will'

Choose the correct sentence to predict the future.
